    Types of Personal Information Collected 
    The information we collect depends on how you interact with us, the Services you use, and the choices you make. 
    We collect two types of personal information: identifiable personal information and pseudonymous personal information.  
    Identifiable personal information (e.g., email address, telephone number) may be used to identify an actual person.  
    Pseudonymous personal information (e.g., a mobile ad ID) can’t be used on its own to directly identify a person and typically gets used to recognize a particular browser or device over time. We also take steps to de-identify data as described below. 
    
    How Your Information Is Collected and Used 
    We collect information in a variety of ways to facilitate our Services. First, we are sharing some general information about our approach to collecting, using and sharing data. 
    
    Identifiable Personal Information. We generally collect identifiable personal information when you provide that information to us. For example, you might provide your email address to subscribe to our newsletter, to create your account with us or to ask us a question. We use this type of information for the purpose it was provided, although we may pseudonymize your identifiable information (e.g, via a one-way hash) to render it pseudonymous so we may use the pseudonymous personal information as described in this policy.  
    
    Pseudonymous Personal Information. We automatically collect pseudonymous personal information regarding how users utilize the Website, the App and the Services. We are generally unable to use this type of information to identify an individual user without additional identifiable personal information, but will often use this type of information to drive deeper insights on our users and to facilitate targeted advertising programs.  
    
    Aggregated / De-identified Information. We may de-identify data we collect via the Services, Website and/or App (i.e., by removing all pseudonymous and identifiable personal information from the data set) and will share that data with third-parties with fewer restrictions. Similarly, we aggregate data across thousands of users to create statistics sharing insights on demographics or how our users behave while enjoying the Website, App and/or Services.  
    
    Publicly Displayed Data – Some of the identifiable personal information contained on your Profile and/or via posts you make on the App may be seen by other users of the App and/or the public. We believe that enabling others to see this type of information helps to facilitate interactions and connections between users – and it makes using the Services we offer more enjoyable. We try to make this clear; particularly as you are using the App. We strongly caution all users to be mindful of what they disclose publicly.

DO NOT INCLUDE INFORMATION IN YOUR PROFILE THAT YOU WANT TO KEEP PRIVATE.

    When you use the RolldUp App, we may collect your location to determine your distance from other users through the GPS, Wi-Fi, and/or cellular technology in your mobile device. Your last known location is stored on our servers for the purpose of calculating the relative distance between you and other users – as many of our users communicate locally. We’ve found that users search for other users within a few miles of their location.
